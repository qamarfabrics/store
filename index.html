Why unstitched

Sold by the metre, fit by your tailor

Every length on Qamar Fabrics is unstitched — the way most of Pakistan actually buys fabric. You get the exact fabric quality, dye lot and print run, then take it to your own tailor for a fit that ready-made racks can't match. Meterage is listed on every product page so there's no guessing.
