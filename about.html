Our story

Fabric, before anything else

Qamar Fabrics started as a single storefront selling unstitched lengths cut to order. The idea hasn't changed: sell honest fabric — the right weight, the right dye lot, the right meterage — and let a tailor turn it into clothes that actually fit. No ready-made sizing charts, no guessing what "medium" means this season.

Everything under our Signature, Malmal Edit, Ajrak Line, Noor Festive and Bunyaan Basics names is sourced and finished in Pakistan, priced by the metre, and shipped nationwide with cash-on-delivery for anyone who'd rather pay at the door.

A short fabric guide

LawnLightweight, fine-count cotton for spring and summer. Breathes well, presses easily.
KhaddarHandwoven, heavier cotton for autumn/winter. Warmer than lawn, holds block prints well.
Cotton KarandiBrushed, dense-weave winter cotton — warmer than khaddar, smooth surface.
LinenLoose natural weave, breathable, drapes with texture. Best for warm, humid days.
CambricSmooth, mid-weight cotton for everyday wear across most of the year.
Silk / ChiffonFestive and formal weight, used for shirts, trousers and dupattas alike.
