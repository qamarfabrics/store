Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data Qamar Fabrics collects through this website, why, and how it's handled. It applies to qamarfabrics.example (and its GitHub Pages address) and to the account, order and contact features on the site.

1. What we collect

Account data — if you sign in with email/password or Google, we (via Firebase Authentication) receive your name, email address, and a unique account ID. We don't receive or store your Google password.

Order data — when you check out, we collect your name, phone number, delivery address, city, email, and the items and total of your order, so we can fulfil and deliver it.

Contact form data — your name, email and message, if you write to us through the contact page.

Technical data — like most websites, our hosting (GitHub Pages) and backend (Google Firebase) automatically log things like IP address and browser type for security and performance, separately from anything above.

2. How we use it

  • To process, confirm and deliver your order
  • To let you sign in and view your order history
  • To reply to messages sent through the contact form
  • To send order updates by email, and — only if you opt in via the newsletter form — occasional news about new fabric or sales
  • To keep the site secure and prevent abuse

We don't sell your personal data to anyone.

3. Where it's stored

Account, order and message data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore/Authentication), a third-party service we use as our backend. Google processes this data under its own security and privacy commitments as our data processor. The website's front-end code is hosted on GitHub Pages, which does not itself store your personal submissions.

4. Cookies & local storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember what's in your shopping cart between visits — this stays on your device and isn't sent to us until you check out. Signing in uses standard authentication cookies/tokens set by Firebase Authentication.

5. Your rights

You can ask us to see, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, or to stop sending marketing emails, at any time — contact us using the details below. We'll respond as quickly as we reasonably can.

6. Data retention

We keep order records for as long as needed for accounting, warranty and legal purposes, and account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

7. Children's privacy

This site sells children's clothing fabric but is intended to be used by adults making purchases on a child's behalf. We don't knowingly collect account data directly from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site changes — the "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy, or a request about your data? Reach us via the contact page or at hello@qamarfabrics.example.

This page is a starting template, not legal advice. Before you launch — especially before enabling Google Sign-In in production — have a lawyer confirm it meets Pakistan's data-protection rules and, if you'll have customers elsewhere, regulations like GDPR.
